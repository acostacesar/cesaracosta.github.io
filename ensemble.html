Ensemble(Decision Tree + Neural Network)

Boss: How could we change a non-buyer into a buyer?
Me: Maybe, "discount" works for somebody.
Boss: How to know WHO may become a buyer after receiving a discount?
Me: Build a model to discover people with specific features.
Boss: How to build a model?
Me: Ensemble model (decision tree + neural network) may work.
Why choose DT+NN?
A decision tree model is easy to interpret; on the other hand, a neural network model is like a black box, hard to know the training process. Thus, we take the pros and cons of each method. Combined two Algorithm into an ensemble model
